About the Band

Short, direct, professional, and easy for event organizers to forward internally.

Turning Down Today is a high-energy Chicago-area rock cover band performing classic rock, hard rock, and alternative favorites that audiences know and love.

The band has been playing around the suburbs since high school and brings years of live experience, dependable preparation, and a no-hassle approach that makes things easier for event organizers. Led by vocalist Jonny Untch, Turning Down Today delivers tight musicianship, strong vocals, and a setlist packed with recognizable songs from artists like Led Zeppelin, AC/DC, Aerosmith, Van Halen, Tom Petty, Foo Fighters, Pearl Jam, The Killers, Alice in Chains, and Stone Temple Pilots.

With a repertoire of more than 130 songs and flexible set lengths ranging from 45 minutes to 3 hours, Turning Down Today is a strong fit for festivals, summer concert series, village greens, bars, and community events looking for a professional band that shows up, delivers, and keeps the crowd engaged.
